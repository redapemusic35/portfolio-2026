SCHF001: Philosophy Lab for Human Flourishing – Spring 2026

An Experiential Literature Program in Applied Philosophy through MMOs

Enter the Star Citizen Universe: Pyro & Nyx Ethical Frontier

Quantum Jump to the Frontier of Reality: A lawless Pyro raid on Ruin Station or Nyx smuggling run through Glaciem Ring (ambient Star Citizen Pyro/Nyx gameplay loop – immersive 4K footage of contested zones, asteroid belts, and faction skirmishes)

🎯 Learning Objectives

Hone ethical reasoning via Reality+ simulations in Star Citizen’s Pyro (chaos) & Nyx (rebellion)
Forge moral attachments & virtues (empathy, resilience, self-sufficiency) through ITC-aligned raids & hauls
Master digital literacy & STEAM skills in high-stakes MMO ops for veterans/early career pros
Translate virtual flourishing to real-world impact: Hobbesian wars, Rawlsian justice, Chalmers’ virtual minds

Strap in, pioneer. You’re not just logging into Star Citizen—you’re spawning into David Chalmers’ Reality+: a simulated universe where Pyro’s gang turfs (Xenothreat terrorists, CFP settlers, Headhunters outlaws) test “might vs. right,” and Nyx’s People’s Alliance in Levski debates anti-oligarchy ethics amid Glaciem Ring ambushes.

Your ITC Guild Role: Join as traders/security for Interstellar Trading Corporation (ITCN)—bait pirates, raid contested zones (Orbituary, Checkmate, Ruin for compboards 1-7), smuggle for rebels, hunt Vanduul. Gear up: Heavy Marine Armor, Medium Hercs, ROC miners, fighters.

Every 45-60min event from CRU-L1/Pyro gates or Nyx jump points ends in Discord debrief: “Does chaos breed flourishing?” Roll d20 for choices. Veterans: Channel resilience in flare storms. Early career: Navigate corporate aid to rebels. By week’s end, embody philosophers in pixels—Reality+ blurs sim/reality as you vault exec hangars or evade UEE patrols. Course Text

Core Text (Summer 2026): Chalmers, David. 2022. Reality+: Virtual Worlds and the Problems of Philosophy. W.W. Norton. (Weekly chapters: Ch. 1-2 simulation hypothesis for Pyro spawns; Ch. 3-5 virtual actions in raids; Ch. 6-8 minds/consciousness for Nyx ethics. E-book/PDF excerpts for accessibility.)

Supplementary Materials

  • MMO: Star Citizen Alpha/PTU (free fly events or partnerships for SCHF fleet; focus Pyro/Nyx).
  • Gear/Loadouts: Provided via esports lab/PC donations (Heavy Armor, traders/fighters, ROCs).
  • Tools: Discord for guild/polls, journals, Google Forms (pre/post virtue surveys), Reality+ audio summaries.
  • Philosophy PDFs: Aristotle (eudaimonia), Hobbes/Rawls hooks.

SCHF-Aligned ITC Gameplay in Pyro & Nyx

  • Pyro: Lawless chaos for high-risk raids (contested zones: Orbituary/Checkmate/Ruin).
  • Nyx: Anti-oligarchy smuggling/Vanduul hunts (Levski hub, Glaciem Ring). 45-60min events amplify Reality+ with ITC trading/security + SCHF philosophy.
